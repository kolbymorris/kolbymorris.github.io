Intro

My name is Kolby Morris, I am currently a senior at Texas State University. I will be graduating in May 2023 with a B.A. in Computer Science.

I began college as a business major but quickly fell in love with writing small programs to handle every day tasks. I decided to change my major and pursue Computer Science instead. I have interned at several companies from start-ups to huge corporations. The part of being a software developer that I love is the fact that each day is a chance to tackle a new challenge. The industry is so vast and so anytime I can find something new that catches my interest being able to explore and interact with so many languages, libraries, frameworks etc. is something that I not only find exciting but fulfilling as well.
